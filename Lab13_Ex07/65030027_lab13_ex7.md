- ผลลัพท์ที่ได้ คือ
  - TV Turn on
  - TV Channel up
  - TV Channel down
  - TV Turn off
  - Lamp Turn on
  - Lamp cannot change channel
  - Lamp cannot change channel
  - Lamp Turn off
